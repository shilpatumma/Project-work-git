Diabetes Care

Personalised & Unique Diet Programs for Diabetes Control

A healthy diet plan, physical exercise, lowering
cholesterol levels, and improving personal hygiene are
all essential for people who suffer from diabetes. Merely
taking medicine as prescribed is not sufficient. Avoiding
sugar and sweets alone won’t help. Depending on their
condition, both types of diabetics need to follow the
type 2 diabetes food chart.

The numerous organ systems severely affected by long
-term undiagnosed and untreated diabetes cause high
morbidity and mortality. Primary organs affected
include the kidneys, eyes, and nervous system. Also, we
should avoid the complications of diabetes, especially if
we have not treated it for years. A diabetic diet needs to
be planned after considering the preferences and
dislikes of the patients.

Heart disease cannot be prevented with a ‘magic’ food.

than is necessary to meet our sodium requirements
(salt contains sodium and chloride). The evidence does
suggest, however, that plant foods – particularly
wholegrain cereals, legumes, nuts, fruits and vegetables
– may reduce heart disease risks.
